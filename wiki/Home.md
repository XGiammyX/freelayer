# Welcome to FreeLayer

**Private communication without a central server.**

FreeLayer is an open-source, research-stage project exploring private rooms that live on your devices, sealed capsules that can travel through any transport, and privacy rules enforced by the app core.

> ⚠️ **Foundation stage — not ready for real secrets yet.** No release, no implemented cryptography, no messaging. The [Trust Center](https://github.com/XGiammyX/freelayer/blob/main/docs/TRUST_CENTER.md) is the honest source of truth.

## The idea in three lines

1. A **Sovereign Room** is a private workspace that lives on members' devices — not on a company server.
2. Every update becomes a **sealed capsule** that any courier (relay, file, QR, USB) can carry **without reading it**.
3. A **policy engine in the app core** decides what may be stored, sent, previewed, copied or shown.
